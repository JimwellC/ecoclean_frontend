At EcoClean, we are committed to fostering a cleaner, greener Barangay Santo Cristo by revolutionizing waste management through digital innovation.

About Barangay Santo Cristo

Barangay Santo Cristo is a thriving community known for its strong commitment to sustainability and environmental responsibility. With a rich history and a dedicated population, the barangay continues to evolve by integrating innovative waste management solutions and community-driven initiatives.

As one of the progressive barangays, Santo Cristo aims to enhance its environmental programs, ensuring that every resident plays a role in making the community cleaner and greener for future generations.

Why We Chose Santo Cristo

We selected Barangay Santo Cristo for our EcoClean initiative because of its proactive community engagement and willingness to embrace sustainable solutions. The barangay has shown a strong commitment to improving waste management, making it an ideal partner for this project.

Additionally, the barangay's strategic location and its residents' enthusiasm for environmental programs make it the perfect place to pilot and scale our waste management innovations. We hope that this partnership is a success

Our Mission

We promote sustainable waste management solutions, educating individuals and inspiring action for eco-friendly communities.

Our Vision

We envision a community where every resident takes responsibility for preserving the environment for a healthier future.
